## 🔍 PROCEDURAL DEFICIENCIES

### DGAC Investigation Procedure Flaws
**Current Flawed Procedure:**
1. Crew makes allegation
2. Security activated immediately
3. Captain orders removal based on allegation
4. Passenger removed (force if necessary)
5. Investigation conducted after removal
6. Resolution determined post-facto

**Legal Deficiencies Identified:**
1. **No Presumption of Innocence:** Treated as guilty until proven innocent
2. **No Immediate Evidence Review:** Action before verification
3. **No Witness Confrontation:** Denied right to question accuser
4. **No Independent Assessment:** Relies solely on crew statements
5. **No Preventive Measures:** No checks against false allegations
