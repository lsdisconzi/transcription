## BRAZILIAN LEGAL FRAMEWORKS APPLIED

### 1. CÓDIGO DE DEFESA DO CONSUMIDOR (CDC) - Law 8.078/1990

#### Article 6 - Basic Consumer Rights
- **VIOLATION**: Right to adequate and clear information
  - Evidence: "No hai nada por escrito" (No written documentation)
  - Brazilian Standard: Written explanation required for service denial
  
- **VIOLATION**: Right to protection against abusive practices
  - Evidence: Unilateral removal without proper justification
  - Brazilian Standard: Proportionality and reasonableness required

#### Article 14 - Service Provider Liability
- **VIOLATION**: Liability for damages caused by service defects
  - Evidence: Passenger removal causing travel disruption
  - Brazilian Standard: Strict liability for service providers

#### Article 51 - Abusive Clauses
- **POTENTIAL VIOLATION**: Unilateral modification of contract
  - Evidence: Removal from flight without mutual agreement
  - Brazilian Standard: Contract modification requires consumer consent

### 2. ANAC RESOLUÇÃO Nº 400/2016 - General Conditions of Air Transport

#### Article 10 - Passenger Rights
- **VIOLATION**: Right to information about flight changes
  - Evidence: No written explanation for removal
  - Requirement: Written notification of denied boarding

#### Article 11 - Denied Boarding Compensation
- **APPLICABLE**: International flight denied boarding
  - Evidence: Passenger forcibly removed from flight
  - Compensation: Required under Brazilian regulations

#### Article 12 - Documentation Requirements
- **VIOLATION**: Failure to provide proper documentation
  - Evidence: Refusal to provide written incident report
  - Requirement: Mandatory documentation for security incidents

### 3. RESOLUÇÃO Nº 138/2010 - Passenger Treatment Standards

#### Security Incident Procedures
- **VIOLATION**: Lack of proper incident documentation
- **VIOLATION**: Inadequate evidence handling procedures
- **VIOLATION**: Failure to follow escalation protocols

### 4. RBAC REGULATIONS

#### RBAC 01 - General Aviation Regulations
- **APPLICABLE**: International operations standards
- **REQUIREMENT**: Compliance with Brazilian aviation standards worldwide

#### RBAC 153 - Airport Operations
- **APPLICABLE**: Passenger handling standards
- **REQUIREMENT**: Professional conduct and proper procedures

## SPECIFIC VIOLATIONS UNDER BRAZILIAN LAW

### 1. CONSUMER RIGHTS VIOLATIONS (CDC)

#### Violation 1: Information Rights (CDC Art. 6, IV)
- **Description**: Denial of written explanation for service denial
- **Evidence**: 
  - Passenger request: "¡Por escrito!"
  - Airline response: "No hai nada por escrito"
  - Passenger confirmation: "No hay nada por escrito"
- **Severity**: HIGH
- **Legal Consequence**: Administrative fine + compensation

#### Violation 2: Abusive Practice (CDC Art. 39)
- **Description**: Imposition of disproportionate penalties
- **Evidence**: Removal from flight based on unsubstantiated claims
- **Severity**: HIGH
- **Legal Consequence**: Contract nullification + damages

#### Violation 3: Service Defect Liability (CDC Art. 14)
- **Description**: Failure to provide contracted service
- **Evidence**: Passenger prevented from traveling on purchased flight
- **Severity**: HIGH
- **Legal Consequence**: Full refund + compensation + damages

### 2. AVIATION REGULATION VIOLATIONS (ANAC)

#### Violation 1: Documentation Failure (Res. 400/2016)
- **Description**: Failure to provide denied boarding documentation
- **Evidence**: No written notice provided
- **Severity**: MEDIUM-HIGH
- **Legal Consequence**: Administrative sanctions

#### Violation 2: Passenger Treatment Standards (Res. 138/2010)
- **Description**: Unprofessional conduct and procedure violations
- **Evidence**: Dismissive communication, refusal to escalate
- **Severity**: MEDIUM
- **Legal Consequence**: Training requirements + monitoring

### 3. CONTRACT LAW VIOLATIONS

#### Violation 1: Contract Breach
- **Description**: Failure to fulfill air transport contract
- **Legal Basis**: Brazilian Civil Code Articles 389-420
- **Severity**: HIGH
- **Remedy**: Specific performance or damages

#### Violation 2: Good Faith Violation
- **Description**: Lack of transparency and cooperation
- **Legal Basis**: Civil Code Article 422
- **Severity**: MEDIUM-HIGH
- **Remedy**: Additional damages
