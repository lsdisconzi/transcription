### A. CHILEAN LEGAL FRAMEWORK VIOLATIONS

#### 1. Consumer Protection Law (Ley 19.496) Violations
- **Article 3**: Right to adequate information - **VIOLATED**
  - Evidence: "No hai nada por escrito" (No written documentation)
  - Severity: HIGH
  
- **Article 16**: Prohibition of abusive practices - **VIOLATED**
  - Evidence: Unilateral removal without proper justification
  - Severity: HIGH
  
- **Article 17**: Right to claim and obtain redress - **VIOLATED**
  - Evidence: "Nadie le va a dar una solución" (No one will give you a solution)
  - Severity: HIGH

#### 2. Constitutional Concerns
- **Article 19**: Equality before the law - **POTENTIAL VIOLATION**
  - Evidence: "¿Eso es porque yo soy inmigrante? ... ¿Eso es racismo?"
  - Requires investigation

  - Severity: MEDIUM
  
- **Security Protocols**: Evidence handling - **VIOLATED**
  - Evidence: CCTV footage claimed but not shown
  - Severity: HIGH
