## Incident Summary

This transcript documents the passenger's interaction with DGAC official Nicolas regarding his removal from the flight to Brasilia. The passenger seeks guidance on how to proceed after being forcibly removed from his flight based on false allegations that were later disproven by PDI investigation. The DGAC official provides procedural advice but clarifies jurisdictional limitations.

## Key Issues and Themes

1. **Jurisdictional Limitations**: DGAC clarifies they cannot intervene directly in airline-passenger disputes
2. **Legal Process Guidance**: Passenger advised to pursue judicial process given PDI investigation results
3. **Burden of Proof**: Passenger challenges airline's camera evidence claims and demands proper proof
4. **Documentation Demands**: Continued insistence on written explanations for flight removal
5. **False Allegations**: Two separate false accusations (aggression and boarding pass violation) disproven by PDI
6. **Forceful Removal**: Passenger mentions being removed "a fuerza" (by force) from the flight
7. **Procedural Escalation**: LATAM staff offers to involve security personnel but passenger wants written documentation

## Outcome

- DGAC provides procedural guidance but clarifies jurisdictional limitations
- Passenger advised to pursue judicial process given PDI investigation results
- LATAM staff offers to involve security personnel but passenger insists on written documentation
- No immediate resolution provided for flight rescheduling
- Passenger remains determined to obtain proper written explanations
- Legal process appears to be the recommended next step
